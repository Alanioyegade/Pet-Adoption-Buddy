Services We Offer

1. Pet Adoption

We connect loving families with pets in need. Our adoption process includes:

  • Browse Adoptable Pets: Explore our online listings of dogs, cats, rabbits, and other animals available for adoption.
  • Application Process: Fill out our simple online adoption application to express interest in a pet.
  • Meet-and-Greet: Schedule a meeting with your chosen pet to ensure a good match.
  • Home Visit/Interview: In some cases, we may conduct a home visit or interview to ensure your home is a safe environment for the pet.
  • Adoption Support: Our team provides guidance and support throughout the adoption process, from selecting the right pet to the final adoption step.

2. Foster Program

Our Foster-to-Adopt program allows potential adopters to take a pet home temporarily before making a permanent commitment. This gives families the opportunity to ensure the pet is a good fit for their home and lifestyle.

  • Short-Term Fostering: If you're unable to adopt immediately, consider fostering a pet. You'll provide a temporary loving home while we work to find a permanent family.
  • Foster Support: We provide all necessary supplies and veterinary care for foster pets, plus ongoing support to ensure a successful fostering experience.

3. Pet Transport Services

We offer pet transport services for families adopting pets from other areas or states. This service helps ensure a safe and smooth journey for the pet, so they can join their new family without stress.

  • Nationwide Transport: Whether you’re adopting locally or across state lines, we can arrange transport for your new pet.
  • Safe & Comfortable Travel: Our transportation service ensures pets are safely and comfortably transported to their new homes.

4. Veterinary Care & Wellness

We prioritize the health and wellness of all the pets in our care. Our services include:

  • Vaccinations & Spaying/Neutering: All pets up for adoption are fully vaccinated and spayed/neutered to ensure they’re healthy and ready for a new home.
  • Health Check-ups: Each pet undergoes a thorough health screening before being listed for adoption.
  • Microchipping: We microchip all adoptable pets to ensure they are easily identified in case they get lost after adoption.

5. Training & Behavior Support

We understand that transitioning into a new home can be stressful for pets, and we’re here to help. Our training and behavior support services include:

  • Basic Training Resources: We offer tips and guides for basic obedience and socialization.
  • Behavioral Consultations: For pets with special behavioral needs, we provide consultations to address issues such as separation anxiety, house training, and aggression.
  • Post-Adoption Support: Our team is available to provide ongoing support after adoption to help with training and behavior challenges.

6. Pet Supplies & Adoption Packages

We offer pet adoption packages that include essential supplies to help you get started with your new pet. These packages may include:

  • Food & Bowls
  • Collars, Leashes, and Harnesses
  • Beds & Blankets
  • Toys & Chew Items

These packages ensure that adopters have everything they need to welcome their new pet into their home.

7. Donations & Fundraising

We rely on the generosity of people like you to help fund our mission of rescuing and rehoming pets. Your donations go directly to:

  • Covering veterinary care costs.
  • Funding transport and fostering programs.
  • Providing food, supplies, and shelter for our animals.

We also organize fundraising events throughout the year and offer opportunities for individuals and businesses to get involved.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I adopt a pet?

Adopting a pet is easy! Just browse our list of available pets, fill out the adoption application, and schedule a meet-and-greet with the pet you're interested in. Our team will guide you through the entire process.

2. Do I need to be a local resident to adopt?

While we prioritize local adoptions, we also offer pet transport services for families adopting from other areas or states. Please contact us for more details.

3. What’s the adoption fee?

The adoption fee varies depending on the pet’s breed, age, and health status. Fees typically cover vaccinations, spaying/neutering, microchipping, and general veterinary care.

4. Can I foster a pet before adopting?

Yes, we offer a Foster-to-Adopt program, allowing you to take a pet home temporarily to ensure they are a good fit for your family before making a permanent adoption commitment.

5. What is the process for surrendering a pet?

If you need to surrender your pet, please reach out to us directly. We’ll assess your pet’s needs and find them a new, loving home through our adoption program.

6. Are there any restrictions for adoption?

We review adoption applications based on the pet’s specific needs and the adopter’s lifestyle. Some pets may have certain requirements (e.g., no other pets, fenced yard, etc.). Each case is reviewed individually.

7. Can I donate to support your mission?

Yes! We rely on the generosity of our supporters. You can make a one-time or recurring donation through our website, or get involved in our fundraising events. All donations go directly to the care of our pets.

8. What happens if I need to return a pet after adoption?

If for any reason you are unable to keep the pet, please contact us immediately. We’ll work with you to find a solution, whether it’s a return or helping with training and behavior support.

9. Do you provide training for adopted pets?

Yes! We offer basic training resources and post-adoption support for pets, including behavioral consultations and training tips to ensure a smooth transition into your home.

10. How can I get involved as a volunteer?

We welcome volunteers! You can help by fostering pets, assisting with adoption events, or providing administrative support. Please visit our volunteer page for more information on how to get involved.
